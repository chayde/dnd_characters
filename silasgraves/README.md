# Silas Graves
### Rock Gnome Necromancer (Level 7) | Curse of Strahd

![Silas Graves Portrait](Silas_Graves.png)

> *"Death is not an end, but a transition. Time is the only true currency, and I merely seek to extend it for those who still have a purpose to serve."*

## Character Overview
Silas Graves is a benevolent necromancer and professional undertaker currently traversing the mists of Barovia. Unlike the stereotypical power-hungry wizard, Silas views his magic as a tool for preservation and dignity. He treats the dead with immense respect, viewing his animated companions not as tools, but as souls granted extended time to fulfill their unfinished business.

*   **Class:** Wizard (School of Necromancy)
*   **Race:** Rock Gnome (2024 Rules)
*   **Background:** Sage (Mortician/Researcher)
*   **Alignment:** Neutral Good
*   **Current HP:** 51
*   **Spell Save DC:** 15

## The Retinue (Companions)
Silas is rarely alone, traveling with a "family" of preserved spirits:

*   **Ink (Familiar):** A raven who acts as Silas's eyes from above.
*   **Rose & Thorn Durst (Skeletons):** The tragic children of the Death House, given new forms to protect the party.
    *   *Stats:* AC 13, HP 20, Shortbows (+5 hit, 1d6+5 dmg).
*   **Mook (Zombie):** A former Revenant defeated in battle, now serving as the group's indomitable shield.
    *   *Stats:* AC 8, HP 29, Undead Fortitude.
