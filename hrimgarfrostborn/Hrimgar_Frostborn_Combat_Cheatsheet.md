## OPENING ROUND FLOWCHART

### VS MULTIPLE ENEMIES (3+) - THE BLIZZARD
**Best Opening (Maximum Damage Path):**
1. **Action:** Cast **Conjure Minor Elementals** (4th-level slot)
   - Creates a 15 ft. aura of swirling cold elementals around you
   - Adds **2d8 cold damage** to EVERY attack you make for 10 minutes
   - Ground becomes difficult terrain for enemies
2. **Bonus Action:** **Wrath of the Sea**
   - Hit one enemy in 5 ft. for **2d6 cold damage** and push 15 ft.
3. **Movement:** Position near multiple enemies
4. **Next Turn:** Attack 3 times with +2d8 cold on each attack

---

### VS SINGLE TOUGH ENEMY (Boss) - THE GLACIER
**Best Opening:**
1. **Action:** Cast **Conjure Minor Elementals** (4th-level slot)
2. **Bonus Action:** **Wrath of the Sea** (damage + push)
3. **Next Turn:** Attack 3 times
   - Add **Frost's Chill** to one attack for extra 1d6 cold
   - Add **Primal Strike** to one attack for extra 1d8 cold
   - Total per turn: 3 attacks + 6d8 cold + 1d8 cold + 1d6 cold

---

### WHEN YOU NEED TO BE HUGE - THE COLOSSUS
**For Strength Checks, Grappling, or Intimidation:**
1. **Bonus Action:** **Large Form** (become Large - 14 ft. tall, +10 ft. speed, Advantage on STR checks)
2. **Action:** Cast **Enlarge/Reduce** (choose Enlarge)
   - Become **Huge** (28 ft. tall, ~19,000 lbs.)
   - Weapons deal +1d4 damage
   - Count as Gargantuan for lifting (4,320 lbs. carrying capacity!)
3. **Movement:** Use your 40 ft. speed (Large Form bonus)
4. **Next Turn:** Attack 3 times with +1d4 damage from Enlarge

**IMPORTANT:** You MUST use Large Form BEFORE Enlarge/Reduce, or you'll only get to Large size!

## IMPORTANT REMINDERS

### ‚úÖ ALWAYS REMEMBER
- **You attack THREE times per turn** (Attack action x2 with Nick + Dual Wielder bonus action)
- **Conjure Minor Elementals lasts 10 minutes** - You can pre-cast it if you know a fight is coming
- **Primal Strike adds 1d8 cold to ONE attack per turn** - Always use this!
- **Large Form must come BEFORE Enlarge** - Wrong order = you only become Large, not Huge
- **Weapon Mastery is always on:**
  - Hand Axe (Vex): Hit = advantage on next attack against that target
  - Light Hammer (Nick): Make extra attack as part of Attack action (already built into your 3 attacks)

### ‚ö†Ô∏è DON'T FORGET
- **Concentration:** Only one concentration spell at a time
  - Conjure Minor Elementals vs Enlarge/Reduce (can't have both!)
  - Wrath of the Sea does NOT require concentration (it's a feature, not a spell)
- **Wrath of the Sea uses Wild Shape charges** - You have 3 total
- **Large Form is 1/long rest** - Don't waste it on trivial encounters
- **Frost's Chill is limited** - Only 4 uses per long rest
- **Two-Weapon Fighting Style:** Your Light Hammer bonus action adds STR modifier to damage (already included in calculations)

### üéØ TACTICAL TIPS
- **Pre-cast Conjure Minor Elementals** before fights if you have 1 minute warning
- **Use Vex mastery strategically:** Attack with Hand Axe first to get advantage on your second Hand Axe attack
- **Save your 4th-level slots for Conjure Minor Elementals** - it's your best damage multiplier
- **Wrath of the Sea pushes 15 ft.** - Use it to shove enemies off cliffs or into hazards
- **Going Huge takes 2 turns of setup** - Plan accordingly, or just use damage mode
- **Guidance is free** - Cast it on allies making important checks (lockpicking, Perception, etc.)
- **Wild Shape for scouting:** Turn into an Owl and fly ahead to scout, then report back

## COMMON COMBAT SCENARIOS

### "We Have 1 Minute Before Combat Starts"
1. **Action:** Cast **Conjure Minor Elementals** (lasts 10 minutes)
2. **Walk into combat as a swirling blizzard**
3. **Turn 1 of combat:** Attack 3 times immediately with +2d8 cold on each

### "Surprise Round / Ambush"
1. **Bonus Action:** **Wrath of the Sea** (damage + push priority target)
2. **Action:** Attack 3 times (you're not buffed yet, but you're making the most of surprise)
3. **Next Turn:** Cast Conjure Minor Elementals

### "I Need to Become HUGE Right Now"
1. **Turn 1 - Bonus Action:** Large Form (become Large)
2. **Turn 1 - Action:** Enlarge/Reduce (become Huge)
3. **Turn 2+:** Attack 3 times with +1d4 damage
4. **Remember:** You're concentrating on Enlarge, so no Conjure Minor Elementals!

### "The Party is Spread Out and Needs Stealth"
1. **Before scouting:** Cast **Pass Without Trace** (+10 to everyone's Stealth)
2. **Wild Shape into an Owl** (60 ft. fly speed, darkvision)
3. **Scout ahead, then report back**

### "Someone is Dying"
1. **Action:** **Cure Wounds** (1d8+2 healing) - more if you use higher slots
2. **Bonus Action:** **Healing Word** (1d4+2 healing, ranged) - if you didn't attack
3. **After combat:** Cast **Goodberry** for efficient healing (10 HP for a 1st-level slot)

### "Enemy is Faster Than Me"
1. **Hit them with Frost's Chill** (-10 ft. speed + damage)
2. **Push them with Wrath of the Sea** (15 ft. push)
3. **Cast Entangle/Spike Growth** (if prepared) to slow them down
