## COMMON COMBAT SCENARIOS

### "I Need to Kill This Enemy NOW"
1. **Guided Strike** (+10 to hit) to guarantee you hit
2. **Divine Smite** with highest spell slot available
3. Add **Frost's Chill** if they're still standing

### "I'm Surrounded!"
1. **Conquering Presence** (Channel Divinity) - frighten everyone
2. Use **Cleave** to hit two enemies per turn
3. **Stone's Endurance** if you take a big hit

### "My Ally is Dying"
1. **Lay on Hands** (bonus action) to stabilize or heal
2. If you have actions left, attack to eliminate the threat
3. Cast **Prayer of Healing** after combat (heals 6 people)

### "I Missed My Attack!"
- **Guided Strike** can be used AFTER you see the roll - turns miss into hit
- Remember: Graze mastery (if using greatsword) still deals 4 damage on miss

### "Enemy is Running Away"
- **Opportunity Attack** as they leave
- If you crit or kill, **Hew** lets you attack again (bonus action)
- Consider if you want to pursue or protect your allies
