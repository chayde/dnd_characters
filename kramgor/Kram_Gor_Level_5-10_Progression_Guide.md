**Goal:** Efficient cleanup, save resources

**1st-Level Slots (4 per day):**
- Use liberally for Wrathful Smite, Bless, Shield of Faith

- Save 1-2 for emergencies (healing, Protection from Evil and Good)

**2nd-Level Slots (2-3 per day):**
- Use for Hold Person (auto-crit combos)

- Use for Prayer of Healing (post-combat)

**3rd-Level Slots (2 per day at level 9-10):**
- **HIGH VALUE** - use carefully
- Spirit Shroud for long fights (10 rounds of +2d8 damage)
- Fear for crowd control emergencies
- Aura of Vitality for massive healing after tough fights
- Don't waste on trash mobs

**Divine Smite Priority:**
1. Use **free Divine Smite** early in adventuring day (on a hit)
2. Save spell slots for critical hits or killing blows
3. VS undead/fiends: Prioritize smites (+1d8 bonus)
4. VS humanoids: Save slots for Hold Person ‚Üí auto-crit ‚Üí double smite dice

### When to Short Rest

**You benefit greatly from short rests:**
- Regain 1 Channel Divinity use
- Regain Stone's Endurance
- Roll hit dice for healing (1d10+2 each)

**Short rest after:**
- You've used both Channel Divinity
- Party is below 75% HP and you're low on spell slots
- Before a known tough fight

**Don't short rest when:**
- You still have both Channel Divinity
- Party is healthy and you have spell slots
- Time pressure in story

---

### When to Long Rest

**Long rest when:**
- Out of 3rd-level spell slots (level 9-10)
- Used free Divine Smite + most spell slots
- Party is badly hurt
- Before major story encounter (boss, big dungeon)

**Can push for one more fight if:**
- You have 2+ spell slots
- Party has healing resources
- Enemies aren't too dangerous
