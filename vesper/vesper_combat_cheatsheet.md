## âš”ï¸ Round 1: The Setup
**Goal:** Establish control or maximize damage immediately.

### Option A: The "Solar Flare" (Maximum Damage)
*   **Action:** **Radiant Consumption** (Scourge Aasimar). You erupt with burning light.
    *   *Effect:* 10ft radius bright light. End of turn: 5 radiant dmg to everyone in 10ft (including you). Attacks deal extra 9 radiant dmg once per turn.
*   **Bonus Action:** **Starry Form (Archer)**.
    *   *Effect:* You can shoot a bolt of light (2d8+5) as a Bonus Action on subsequent turns.
*   **Movement:** Position yourself near enemies to burn them with your aura (but careful of allies).

### Option B: The "Battlefield Control" (Lockdown)
*   **Bonus Action:** **Starry Form (Dragon)**.
    *   *Effect:* Minimum 10 on Con saves (Concentration is nearly unbreakable). Fly 20ft.
*   **Action:** Cast a Big Concentration Spell:
    *   *Wall of Stone:* Split the enemy team, trap the boss, or block a doorway.
    *   *Wall of Thorns* (Staff): 6th-level spell! Massive area damage and restrain.
    *   *Faerie Fire:* If enemies are invisible/hard to hit. Advantage for everyone.
    *   *Entangle / Spike Growth:* Stop them from moving.

### Option C: The "Panic Button" (Defensive)
*   **Action:** *Dodge* or cast *Greater Restoration* if someone is debilitated immediately.
*   **Bonus Action:** **Starry Form (Chalice)**.
    *   *Effect:* Prepare for massive healing next turn.

## ðŸ”„ Sustained Combat (Round 2+)

### If you are in Archer Form:
*   **Bonus Action:** **Starry Archer Shot** (Range 60ft).
    *   *Attack:* +11 to hit.
    *   *Damage:* 2d8+5 Radiant.
*   **Action:** Cast a Blast Spell or Cantrip.
    *   *Guiding Bolt:* 4d6 Radiant + Advantage for next ally.
    *   *Moonbeam:* Move it onto enemies (Action).
    *   *Produce Flame:* 2d8 Fire.
    *   *Staff Attack:* If stuck in melee (+2 Quarterstaff).

### If you are in Chalice Form:
*   **Action:** Cast a Healing Spell.
    *   *Cure Wounds* (Level 1): 1d8+5 healing + **Extra 2d8+5 (Chalice)**.
    *   *Healing Word* (Bonus Action): 1d4+5 healing + **Extra 2d8+5 (Chalice)**.
*   **Result:** You effectively heal two people for the price of one spell slot, or one person for a massive amount.

### If you are in Dragon Form:
*   **Focus:** Maintain Concentration on your big spell (*Wall of Thorns*, *Moonbeam*, etc.).
*   **Action:** Use Cantrips or Non-Concentration spells (*Guiding Bolt*, *Ice Storm*, *Plant Growth*).
*   **Movement:** Use your 20ft Hover speed to stay out of melee reach.

## ðŸª„ Magic Item Tricks
*   **Staff of the Woodlands:**
    *   *Pass Without Trace:* Cast at will. Never turn this off unless you are concentrating on combat spells.
    *   *Wall of Thorns:* You have 6 charges. This costs 6 charges. You can cast this ONCE per day. Make it count. It creates a 60ft long, 10ft high, 5ft thick wall.
*   **Sentinel Shield:**
    *   You have Advantage on Initiative. You will likely go first. Don't panic; set up the battlefield.
*   **Radiant Consumption (Aasimar):**
    *   Don't forget this hurts YOU (5 dmg/turn). Use *Absorb Elements* (if you had it) or just tank it with your high HP. Be careful not to kill unconscious allies with the aura!
